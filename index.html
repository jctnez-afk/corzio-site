Does this work in my city?

+
Yes — Meta Ads and AI follow-up work in any US city. We've run campaigns in major metros and smaller markets. On your demo call, we'll pull up your specific area and show you estimated lead volume and cost per lead. We only take clients in markets where we're confident we can deliver results.

How fast does the AI actually respond?

+
Under 60 seconds from the moment a lead submits the form. It's active 24/7, so it responds at 11pm just as fast as 10am. Most competitors are following up hours later — or not at all. This is the single biggest conversion advantage in local services.

Do I need to manage any of it?

+
No. The system runs automatically. You'll get notified when appointments are booked, and you can check the dashboard anytime — but you don't need to manage follow-up, ads, or scheduling. We handle the optimization. Your job is to show up to the confirmed appointments.

I already run Meta Ads. Can you work with what I have?

+
Yes. We can audit your existing campaigns and either take them over or layer the automated booking system on top. No. The AI is trained to sound like a friendly, professional member of your team. It uses your business name, matches your tone, and handles conversations naturally. Customers regularly don't know they're texting with an AI — they just know someone got back to them fast.

How long until I start seeing results?

+
Most clients are fully live within 2–4 weeks. After that, results typically start showing within the first 30 days. On the demo call, we'll give you a realistic forecast for your specific area — not generic promises. We only move forward when we believe we can deliver.
